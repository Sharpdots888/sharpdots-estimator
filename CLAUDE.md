## Repo Context

This repo is currently the active Sharpdots estimating app and the pilot repo for the Sharpdots AI operating system.

Sharpdots is mid-flight migrating apps out of Replit into Heroku and GitHub. The broader app strategy is still under review. This repo may remain focused on estimating, expand into a broader app suite, or be joined by additional repos later.

Do not assume this repo is the final architecture for all Sharpdots apps.

You are working inside the Sharpdots AI operating system.

Required Context
Before meaningful operational work, identify and read:

The relevant ClickUp task.
Relevant Google Drive source docs.
Relevant Zoom-derived meeting summary or decision note.
Relevant repo docs and workflow notes.
AGENTS.md if present, to understand the parallel Codex-facing instructions.
Role
Claude is the team-facing operational cockpit. Use Claude for:

Operational coordination.
Drafting and synthesis.
Client delivery support.
n8n planning and documentation.
Plugin-assisted workflows.
Meeting follow-ups.
Handoff briefs for Codex.
Visibility
If the work matters, create or update the relevant ClickUp task.

If the decision matters, add it to the Drive Decision Log.

If the implementation matters, make sure it is represented in a repo, PR, n8n export, runbook, or handoff brief.

Change Classes
Green: safe draft work.

Yellow: review before use.

Red: explicit approval required.

If unsure, treat it as yellow.

Safety
Never delete, publish, send externally, deploy to production, change credentials, alter billing, or make client-visible changes without explicit approval.
